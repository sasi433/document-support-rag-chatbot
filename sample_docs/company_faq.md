# Northstar Support Systems - Company FAQ

> This document describes a fictional company created for demonstration purposes.

## About Northstar

Northstar Support Systems provides customer-support software and managed support tools for small and medium-sized businesses. Its services include the Northstar Support Portal, the Beacon support gateway, and optional deployment assistance.

## Support hours

Standard and Pro customers can contact the support team Monday through Friday from 08:00 to 18:00 Central European Time, excluding Swedish public holidays. Requests submitted outside those hours are placed in the queue for the next business day.

Enterprise customers receive 24/7 assistance for Priority 1 incidents. Lower-priority Enterprise requests follow the normal support schedule.

Customers should open requests through the Northstar Support Portal. If the portal is unavailable, they can email `support@northstar.example`.

## Refund policy

A customer may request a refund for their first subscription payment within 14 calendar days of the original purchase. The request must include the account name, purchase date, and invoice number.

Subscription renewals, usage-based charges, and completed professional services are normally non-refundable. Suspected duplicate charges or billing errors are reviewed regardless of when they are reported.

Approved refunds are returned to the original payment method. Northstar processes an approved refund within five business days, although the customer's bank may take additional time to display it.

## Escalating a support request

Customers should reply to the existing support ticket instead of opening a duplicate. An escalation request should include:

- the ticket number;
- the business impact;
- the number of affected users;
- when the problem started;
- recent error messages or relevant logs.

The support lead reviews escalations during support hours. Active platform outages are handled through the incident-management process and do not require customers to open multiple tickets.

## Data and account safety

Northstar employees never ask customers to send passwords, full payment-card numbers, or API secrets in a support ticket. Sensitive values should be removed from screenshots and logs before they are attached.
