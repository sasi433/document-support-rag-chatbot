# Northstar Support Systems - Billing FAQ

> This billing guide describes a fictional company created for demonstration purposes.

## Accepted payment methods

Northstar accepts Visa, Mastercard, and American Express for monthly and annual subscriptions. Approved annual Enterprise accounts may pay by bank transfer using the instructions on their invoice.

Customers should never send full card numbers or online-banking credentials to Northstar. Payment details are entered only through the secure billing page.

## Failed subscription payments

When a subscription payment fails, Northstar emails the account's billing contact and retries the payment after three days and again after seven days. The customer can update the payment method in the billing portal at any time.

If payment is still unsuccessful after 10 days, the subscription may be suspended. Northstar preserves the account data during a 30-day recovery period. Access is restored after the outstanding balance is paid successfully.

## Invoices and receipts

Invoices and payment receipts are available in the billing portal immediately after a successful charge. Each invoice includes the subscription period, taxes, credits, and payment status.

The billing contact can download invoices as PDF files. Requests to correct a company name, billing address, or tax identifier should be submitted within 30 days of the invoice date.

## Refunds and billing errors

The first subscription payment may be eligible for a refund when requested within 14 calendar days of purchase. Renewals, usage charges, and completed professional services are normally non-refundable. The full eligibility rules are described in the Company FAQ.

Duplicate charges and other suspected billing errors are reviewed separately from the standard refund policy.

## Contacting billing support

Email `billing@northstar.example` and include the account name, invoice number, and a short description of the issue. Do not include passwords, complete payment-card numbers, or API secrets.

Billing support operates Monday through Friday from 08:00 to 18:00 Central European Time, excluding Swedish public holidays.
